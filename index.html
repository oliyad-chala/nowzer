Welcome to Nowzer School

Nowzer School is a private School founded in July 2009 in Adama, Ethiopia; 90 Kms away to South East from Addis Ababa, the Capital City. The founder and principal, Mr. Paulos Gemechu, named the school after a visionary leader of the Macha Oromos during the 16th century. The School features exceptional setups and educational facilities. Its luxurious (20000 m2) beautiful school compound, Learning Management System (LMS) and laboratory demonstrations are some of its unique features. We have classes from pre-school KG to grade 12. Our enrollment capacity is now over 3000 students with 80 classrooms. The school also has two libraries and five laboratories. The classroom size is 25 for KGs and 35 for grade classrooms. We have over 120 well-experienced academic and 60 support staff. All our KG classrooms, offices, and labs are connected with wired and wireless networks to assist the learning and management processes.
